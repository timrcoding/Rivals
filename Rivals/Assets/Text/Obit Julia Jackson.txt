Star Tribune: Obit. Julia Jackson, '03
Julia Jackson, a respected community activist and member of staff at the University of Minnesota, passed away unexpectedly on Sunday January 12th.
Having moved with her son from Arkansaw in 1995, Julia quickly established herself as an empathetic and dynamic member of Berean Missionary Baptist Church, as well as selector of Hymns.
A highly valued cleric at UoM, where she undertook high quality transcription services, as well as eventually teaching introductory English to foreign students.
Says her son Luke, "She was a wonderful mother, who did everything she could for me. She gave me the gift of music and never let me forget about where I come from".
She is remembered by Luke and her sister Elizabeth.
