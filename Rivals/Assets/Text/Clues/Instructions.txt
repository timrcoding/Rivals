Instructions
Help complete the story of Josh and Luke by matching chapters in Lucy's book to the correct titles.
Their story is divided into 5 sections, with 6 chapters each.
You can find out the right titles by reading texts, listening to audio extracts, looking at images and more.
You can look at all the titles by clicking on any book chapter to bring up the full list.
When you have found an answer, click on the chapter and then the appropriate title.
The game will let you know when you have got five correct and you will receive new clues.
Finally, this game is date accurate to the time it depicts.
Some clues may refer to real events or places, so you may need to do some detective work outside of the game.
